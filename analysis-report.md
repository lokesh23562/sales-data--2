# Sales Data Analysis Report

## Objective
Analyze sales performance and identify trends.

## Dataset
- Rows: 100
- Columns: 5

## Key Insights
- Electronics category contributes the highest revenue.
- Sales peak during certain months.
- Clothing has steady but lower sales.

## Visual Analysis
- Bar chart compares category performance.
- Line chart shows monthly trend.
- Pie chart shows sales contribution.

## Conclusion
Sales strategy should focus on high-performing categories and peak months.
